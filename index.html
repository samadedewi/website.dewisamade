About Me

My name is Dewi Shinta Samade, but you can call me Wi. I have a fondness for the color black. I am the youngest of three siblings, filled with joy and enthusiasm. Currently, I am 20 years old. I was born and raised in the Talaud Islands, specifically in the city of Melonguane, which we commonly refer to as the "Karakelang" island. This island is also known as Porodisa or the Talaud Islands cluster, bordering the Philippines directly I have spent most of my youth in this city.

I am an active student at Politeknik Negeri Manado, majoring in Electrical Engineering, specifically in the Information Technology program. I am also involved in the Language Club, a student organization on campus. Through this club, I have made many friends from various regions. I hope that during my time at this campus, I will gain valuable knowledge and skills that will serve as a foundation for my future.
